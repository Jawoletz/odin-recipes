Smoothie

Delicious and Nutritious Smoothie

A simple smoothie to start your day!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups milk or water
  • 2 cups frozen blueberries

Steps

  1. Pour liquid into blender
  2. With blender on low slowly add frozen blueberries
  3. Secure lid of blender and turn to high for 20 seconds
  4. Serve cold and enjoy
