Quick and Easy Dough

This dough has only four ingredients and takes under an hour from start to finish.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups flour
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 Tbsp yeast
  • 2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl
  3. Slowly add water and combine with hands
  4. Knead dough for 5-8 minutes until tacky
  5. Leave dough to rise for 30-60 minutes
  6. Shape dough into a boule and dust lightly with flour
  7. Prepare baking sheet and let dough rest for 5 minute
  8. Bake loaf for 20-25 minutes until golden brown
